Connect LinkedIn Account

LinkedIn Authentication Required

AIHawk needs access to your LinkedIn account to search and apply for jobs on your behalf.

How It Works

When you start a job search, AIHawk will open a browser window where you'll need to log in to your LinkedIn account. This is a one-time process for each session.

Your LinkedIn credentials are never stored on our servers. We use a secure browser session that you control.

Authentication Steps
  1. Click the "Start Job Search" button on your dashboard or job configuration page
  2. A browser window will open and navigate to LinkedIn
  3. Log in with your LinkedIn credentials
  4. Complete any security verification if prompted
  5. Once logged in, AIHawk will automatically start searching and applying for jobs based on your configuration
Important Notes
  • Do not close the browser window during the job application process
  • LinkedIn may occasionally require additional verification
  • For best results, ensure your LinkedIn profile is complete and up-to-date
